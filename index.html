Paraíba, Brazil

João Pessoa is a coastal city located near the mouth of the Paraíba River in eastern Brazil. Its beautiful coast is one of the main attractions, with warm, clear water beaches which enchant residents and tourists with its natural beauty. The João Pessoa's shore is known for its tourist infrastructure, with wooded sidewalks, bikeways,s and restaurants that offer the best of local cooking. Moreover, the city is famous for being one of the greenest cities in the world, with a large area of Mata Atlântica preserved, providing close contact with nature.
